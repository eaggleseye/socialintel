Pricing questions

Can I cancel anytime? ▼
Yes. Cancel at any time with no fees. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period, then you drop back to Free automatically.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We use Paystack, which accepts Nigerian debit cards, Mastercard, Visa, bank transfers and USSD. All prices are in Naira (NGN).
Do you offer refunds? ▼
Yes, within 48 hours of payment if you have used fewer than 5 scans. Contact us to request one.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? ▼
Upgrades apply immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
