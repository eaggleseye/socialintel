Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SocialIntel. Can't find your answer? Contact us.

Getting Started
What is SocialIntel? ▼
SocialIntel is a social media intelligence platform that scans Reddit, X (Twitter), YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and Facebook to surface trending topics, demand gaps, weak signals, unspoken fears and content opportunities -- powered by AI. It tells you exactly what your audience wants before your competitors figure it out.
How do I sign up? ▼
Click "Get started free" on the homepage and sign in with your Google account. No credit card is needed. You will be on the Free plan immediately and can upgrade anytime from your dashboard.
Do I need technical knowledge to use SocialIntel? ▼
Not at all. SocialIntel is built for creators, marketers and business owners -- not developers. Just sign in, type your topic, select your platforms and hit scan. The AI does everything else.
What devices can I use SocialIntel on? ▼
SocialIntel works on any device with a browser -- mobile, tablet or desktop. It is fully optimised for mobile use since most of our users are on their phones.
Is there a free trial? ▼
Yes -- the Free plan is free forever, not just a trial. You get Reddit scanning with 3 scans per day and 25 posts per scan at no cost. You only upgrade when you want more platforms or higher limits.
Plans and Billing
What are the available plans? ▼
We have three plans:

Free -- Reddit only, 3 scans/day, 25 posts per scan, all 6 analysis tabs. Always free.

Starter (₦6,000/mo) -- Reddit + X, 10 scans/day, 50 posts, export to Markdown, location filter.

Pro (₦14,000/mo) -- All 6 platforms, unlimited scans, 100 posts, Content Ideas generator, everything unlocked.
How do I upgrade my plan? ▼
Once signed in, click the "Upgrade" button in the top navigation bar. Select Starter or Pro, then complete payment via Paystack. Your account upgrades instantly after payment.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We use Paystack for payments, which accepts Nigerian debit cards, Mastercard, Visa, bank transfers and USSD. Payment is in Nigerian Naira (NGN).
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes. You can cancel anytime. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. After that you return to the Free plan automatically. There are no cancellation fees.
Will my subscription auto-renew? ▼
Yes, subscriptions renew monthly unless you cancel before the renewal date. You will receive an email reminder before each renewal.
Do you offer refunds? ▼
We offer refunds within 48 hours of payment if you have not used more than 5 scans on your paid plan. Contact us at our contact page to request one.
Can I switch between Starter and Pro? ▼
Yes. You can upgrade from Starter to Pro anytime from your dashboard. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
Are prices in USD or Naira? ▼
All prices are in Nigerian Naira (NGN). Starter is ₦6,000/month and Pro is ₦14,000/month. This makes SocialIntel one of the most affordable intelligence tools available for African creators and businesses.
Platforms and Scanning
Which platforms does SocialIntel scan? ▼
SocialIntel scans Reddit, X (Twitter), YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and Facebook. Platform availability depends on your plan -- Free gets Reddit only, Starter gets Reddit and X, Pro gets all 6.
Why can I only access Reddit on the Free plan? ▼
Reddit is the most information-dense platform for discussions, questions and trends -- making it the best single platform for intelligence gathering. Additional platforms like YouTube, TikTok and Instagram require higher API costs which are covered by paid plans.
How many scans can I do per day? ▼
Free: 3 scans per day. Starter: 10 scans per day. Pro: Unlimited scans. The scan counter resets at midnight every day.
How long does a scan take? ▼
A typical scan takes between 30 seconds and 3 minutes depending on how many platforms you scan and how many posts per scan you select. Reddit-only scans are the fastest.
Can I scan multiple topics at once? ▼
Yes. You can add multiple topics in the Topics of Interest field -- for example "crypto", "Nigerian stocks", and "investment tips" -- and SocialIntel will combine them into one scan.
Can I filter by location? ▼
Yes -- Starter and Pro users can add a location filter (e.g. Nigeria, UK, US) to make results more relevant to a specific market. Free users can upgrade to access this feature.
How recent is the data? ▼
You can choose the timeframe for each scan -- past 24 hours, past 7 days, or past 30 days. This lets you see both breaking trends and longer-term patterns.
AI Features and Results
What are the 6 analysis tabs? ▼
Trending -- Topics with heat ranking (Hot, Warm, Emerging)
Hot Tickers -- Stocks and assets with bullish/bearish sentiment
How-tos -- Questions people are asking, great for content ideas
Weak Signals -- Early topics gaining momentum before going mainstream
Unspoken Fears -- What your audience is anxious about but won't say directly
Demand Gaps -- What people search for daily with zero good content answering them
What is the Content Ideas generator? ▼
Available on Pro only. Click "Generate Content Ideas" on any result card and SocialIntel AI will produce: 5 scroll-stopping hooks, 3 full social media captions with hashtags, a complete 60-second video script, and a 5-day content calendar -- all based on that specific topic.
Can I export my results? ▼
Yes -- Starter and Pro users can export their full scan results as a Markdown report (.md file) using the Export button at the bottom of the results page.
How accurate is the AI analysis? ▼
SocialIntel uses advanced AI to analyse real posts and discussions from the platforms you scan. The results are grounded in actual data, not guesses. However, AI analysis is a guide -- always apply your own judgement and knowledge of your audience.
Does SocialIntel show real post examples? ▼
Yes. The AI surfaces insights drawn from real posts, including engagement scores and the platforms they came from. You can see which platforms a trend is appearing on using the source badges on each card.
Account and Privacy
How do I sign in? ▼
SocialIntel uses Google Sign-In. Click "Continue with Google" on the homepage and sign in with any Google account. No separate password is needed.
Is my data safe? ▼
Yes. We never sell or share your personal data. Your searches and results are private to your account only. We use Firebase Authentication and Firestore which are enterprise-grade secure services by Google.
Can I delete my account? ▼
Yes. Contact us and we will delete your account and all associated data within 48 hours.
Can I share my account with someone else? ▼
Each account is for individual use. Sharing accounts is not allowed as scan limits are per account. If you need multiple seats, contact us for a team arrangement.
Do you use my search history for advertising? ▼
No. Your search topics and scan results are never used for advertising or sold to third parties. We use this data only to provide your results.
Troubleshooting
My scan is taking too long -- what should I do? ▼
Scans can take up to 3 minutes when scanning multiple platforms. If a scan runs longer than 5 minutes, try refreshing the page and running again with fewer platforms selected. Reddit-only scans are fastest.
I upgraded but my account still shows Free -- what happened? ▼
Try signing out and signing back in. Your tier updates automatically after payment but occasionally needs a refresh to display. If the issue persists after 10 minutes, contact us with your payment reference.
I'm getting an error when I try to scan -- how do I fix it? ▼
First check your internet connection. If the error mentions "API" or "fetch failed", try again in a few minutes as the platform APIs may be temporarily slow. If the error persists, contact us with a screenshot.
The Google Sign-In isn't working -- what should I do? ▼
Make sure your browser allows pop-ups from our site. If using Chrome on Android, try opening the site in a regular tab (not incognito). If the problem continues, try a different browser or contact us.
I can see results from platforms I should not have access to -- is this normal? ▼
No, this should not happen. Each plan strictly limits which platforms are scanned. If you see results from platforms outside your plan, please report this to us immediately so we can fix it.
