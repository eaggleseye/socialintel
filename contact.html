Frequently Asked Questions
How do I upgrade my plan? ▼
Once you are signed in, click the "Upgrade" button in the top right corner of the dashboard. You will be taken to a secure Paystack checkout to complete your subscription.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime? ▼
Yes. You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
Why am I seeing limited results on the free tier? ▼
The free tier gives you access to Reddit only with up to 3 scans per day and 25 posts per scan. Upgrade to Starter or Pro to unlock more platforms and higher limits.
What platforms does SocialIntel scan? ▼
We scan Reddit, X (Twitter), YouTube, TikTok, Instagram and Facebook. Platform availability depends on your subscription tier.
Is my data safe? ▼
Yes. We never sell or share your personal data. Your searches and results are private to your account only.
